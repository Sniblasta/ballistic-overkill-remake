Sadly you can't play with players that already own the game,they have to redownload the game from the link down below or modify the game files with the Auto Steam Fix Tools v2 but WE DON'T RECOMMEND CHANGING YOUR MAIN GAME FILES!!!     What made you guys start this project

  • This game was my childhood, when I discovered that this game has moved on steam, I decided to buy it just for the nostalgia.
    I purchased the game 2 months after it's closure. I gifted the game to my friends but like every other thing in life, my friends stopped playing it.
    2 years after the Delisting of the game off steam, I met Gust0p and I screenshared the game throughout Discord. He liked the game but he was
    disappointed when he realised that the game was delisted and if he'd like to purchase the game,it would be too expensive from a 3rd party site.
    So we worked on a non steam - pirated version of the game so me and Gust0p can play together. We shared our job with our friends and it worked.
    So why we decided to share it online? Well it's because me and our friends thought that sharing this game would make people that used to play
    back in the day to return for a "new" experience and to bring new people into the game that haven't heard of it.
